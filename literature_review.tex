\section{Literature Review}

In this section, we plan to organize the literature review into four major themes: (1) foundational transformer-based LLM architectures, (2) empirical studies analyzing hallucination behavior, (3) evaluation benchmarks and detection techniques, and (4) mitigation strategies including retrieval augmentation and alignment methods.

We plan to cover the following key papers \cite{vaswani2017attention,brown2020language,touvron2023llama,ji2023survey,zhang2023survey,shuster2021retrieval,lewis2020rag,ouyang2022rlhf,lin2021truthfulqa,mialon2023augmented}. These works include foundational transformer architecture, GPT models, LLaMA, hallucination surveys, TruthfulQA benchmark, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and alignment techniques such as Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF). The literature review will critically analyze their contributions, limitations, and gaps that motivate further research.
